Privacy Policy

GPT for Sheets (“we”, “our”, “us”) built the GPT for Sheets add‑on as a free add‑on for Google Sheets. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or store any of your spreadsheet data. All processing of your prompts and responses occurs directly via the OpenAI API.

2. How Data Is Transmitted

When you invoke the =GPT() function, your prompt and any referenced cell content are sent to https://api.openai.com using UrlFetchApp. Responses are returned directly to your sheet. We do not log or cache any requests or responses.

3. Security

All communication between Google Sheets and OpenAI’s servers is encrypted over HTTPS. You remain responsible for safeguarding your own OpenAI API key, which you enter into cell B2.

4. Third‑Party Services

This add‑on uses the OpenAI API. Please review OpenAI’s Privacy Policy for details on their data practices.

5. Children

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
